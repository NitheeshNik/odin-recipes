CURD RICE

Ingredients

  • Rice: 1 cup rice (steamed/boiled)
  • Curd: 1 cup thick yogurt (whisked)
  • Milk: 1/4 to 1/2 cup (boiled and cooled; prevents souring)
  • Tempering: 2 tsp oil or ghee, 1/2 tsp mustard seeds, 1 tsp urad dal, 1-2 dried red chilies, a few curry leaves, 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing), 1 tsp grated ginger, 1-2 green chilies
  • Others: Salt to taste, pomegranate seeds, coriander leaves, grated carrots
